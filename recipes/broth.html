How to Make First Class Chef’s Basic Broths - Sanji’s Chicken Stock

Description

If you use it as a base for curry or pot-au-feu, the deliciousness doubles. Lasts two days in an airtight container in the refrigerator, and one week if frozen.

Ingredients

  • 2 wings of chicken bones
  • 3000ml water
  • 150g vegetable bits

Steps

  1. Rinse chicken with water and divide into 3 even pieces.
  2. Put the rinsed chicken into boiling water, when the surface becomes white, remove from water. Clean off any blood, etc
  3. Add water, chicken, and vegetables in a pot and heat on medium. When it reaches a boil, scoop froth from the top
  4. Simmer 40-60 minutes on low
  5. Taste. If the taste is like broth, it is done. Strain out vegetables bits and chicken
