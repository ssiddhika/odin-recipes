Fruit Macedonia of Apology

Description

Adult Fruit Punch!

Ingredients

  • 1 orange
  • 200g pineapple
  • 6 strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1-2tbsp lemon juice
  • 30mL liquor
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • Mint (as needed)

Steps

  1. Peel the orange. With a kitchen knife, remove the seeds from each section (A). Squeeze and save juice. Peel pineapple and remove core. Cut to bite size pieces. Remove strawberry stems and cut in half.
  2. Place 1's fruit in a bowl. Pour orange juice, lemon juice, liquor, and sugar over fruit. Mix. Chill in refrigerator for 1-2 hours.
  3. Just before eating, cut banana into 1 cm thick slices, and add. Garnish with mint.
