Oda Sensei's Favourite Sea Chicken Onigiri

Description

Making these onigiri treasures doesn't take long

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1(80g) can of tuna
  • 1 tbsp miso
  • 1/3 tsp sugar
  • 5cm piece green onion
  • nori (as needed)
  • salt (as needed)

Steps

  1. Finely chop the green onion. Drain the tuna and put it in a frying pan over medium heat, along with the miso and sugar. Mix. Add onion and mix. Remove from heat (A)
  2. Dip hands in lightly salted water, grab a ball of rice and insert 1's ingredients in the middle. Close. Shape into triangular onigiri form. Cut and attach nori as desired.
